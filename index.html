Unlocking Uganda's Mineral Wealth for Sustainable Growth and Prosperity.

We are Uganda’s leading minerals and resources hub, dedicated to sustainable development, investment, and community empowerment. We connect investors and partners with opportunities, promote sustainability and impact, and drive innovation in the minerals sector.

Empowering Uganda’s Mineral Future

We are committed to unlocking the full potential of Uganda’s mineral wealth for the benefit of all. Our mission is to foster a transparent, innovative, and sustainable minerals sector that drives economic growth, creates jobs, and uplifts communities. From gold and copper to rare earths and industrial minerals, we support responsible exploration, investment, and value addition at every stage.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Resource Mapping & Exploration: Advancing geological surveys and data-driven discovery.
  • Investment & Partnerships: Connecting investors with high-potential projects and local expertise.
  • Sustainability: Promoting best practices in environmental protection, safety, and community engagement.
  • Value Addition: Supporting local processing, manufacturing, and export of mineral products.
