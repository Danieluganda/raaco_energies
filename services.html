Discover Uganda's mineral wealth and land resources, their economic impact, and our journey toward sustainable development.

Resource Pillars

Gold

Uganda's leading mineral export, found in Busia, Buhweju, and Karamoja.

Tin

Extensive deposits in Kabale and Kisoro, vital for alloys and electronics.

Limestone

Abundant in Hima, Tororo, and Moroto, key for cement and construction.

Rare Earths

Emerging sector, especially in Busoga and eastern Uganda.

Resource Milestones

  • 2003

    Mining Act Enacted

    Uganda passed the Mining Act to regulate mineral exploration and ensure environmental protection.
  • 2012

    Discovery of Rare Earths

    Significant rare earth element deposits discovered in eastern Uganda, opening new economic opportunities.
  • 2020

    National Land Policy Review

    Uganda reviewed its land policy to strengthen land rights and promote sustainable land use.
