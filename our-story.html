Our Impact in Action

Outbox was founded with a vision to create an inclusive, impactful tech and innovation hub in Uganda, dedicated to empowering entrepreneurs, fostering collaboration, and driving positive change. Since our inception, we have brought together a vibrant community of innovators, partners, and social change-makers.

Our Story

Our Journey

  • 2020

    Foundation & Early Growth

    Outbox was established to create a collaborative environment for startups and social enterprises. We began building partnerships and developing programs to support the local innovation ecosystem.
  • 2022

    Program Expansion

    We expanded our programs to include accelerator initiatives, mentorship programs, and community outreach. Our impact grew as more entrepreneurs joined our ecosystem.
  • 2023

    Strategic Partnerships

    We formed strategic partnerships with international organizations including UNCDF and the Mastercard Foundation, enabling us to scale our impact and reach more entrepreneurs across Uganda.
