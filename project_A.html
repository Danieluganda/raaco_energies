Overview

The Uganda Energy & Minerals Initiative brings together government, communities, and investors to unlock the country’s energy and mineral wealth while ensuring environmental protection and social responsibility.

We focus on responsible energy production, sustainable mining, land use planning, capacity building, and community engagement. Our goal is to create jobs, foster innovation, and drive inclusive growth in Uganda’s energy and minerals sector.

Background

Uganda is endowed with abundant energy resources and a rich variety of minerals. Sustainable development requires balancing economic opportunity with environmental stewardship and community well-being. Our initiative addresses these challenges by promoting best practices, supporting research, and facilitating investment in the sector.

We work to ensure that energy production and mineral extraction benefit all Ugandans, now and for future generations. Our approach includes transparent governance, technology adoption, and partnerships with local and international stakeholders.

What are we looking for

We are seeking innovators, organizations, and communities working in two main areas:

1. Sustainable Energy Solutions

Projects and organizations that advance access to clean, affordable, and reliable energy for Ugandan communities. This includes:

  1. Renewable energy generation (solar, hydro, wind, biomass)
  2. Energy efficiency and conservation initiatives
  3. Off-grid and rural electrification projects
  4. Capacity building and technical training in the energy sector

2. Responsible Minerals Development

Initiatives that promote responsible exploration, extraction, and management of Uganda’s mineral resources. This includes:

  • Mineral mapping and geological surveys
  • Community engagement and benefit sharing
  • Environmental protection and restoration
  • Transparency, traceability, and anti-corruption efforts
  • Skills development for mining communities

How We Work

Our approach combines research, capacity building, technology, and partnerships to deliver impact:

  • Baseline studies and resource mapping
  • Training for energy and mining professionals, landowners, and local leaders
  • Implementation of sustainable energy and mining practices
  • Community workshops and outreach
  • Monitoring, evaluation, and transparent reporting
  • Facilitating investment and market access

The initiative delivers tangible benefits for communities, the environment, and investors.
